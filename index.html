Our Story

A Local Landmark Built on Good Food & Good Company

Samson's is a family-owned bar and restaurant nestled in the heart of Castleton-on-Hudson, New York. Since opening our doors in 2010, we've been the go-to spot for neighbors, families, and friends looking for honest, delicious comfort food and a welcoming atmosphere.

We take pride in sourcing fresh, carefully selected ingredients — many from local farms right here in the Hudson Valley — and turning them into the kind of meals that keep you coming back.

Whether you're stopping in for a bowl of homemade soup, a cold beer after work, or a full dinner with the family, there's always a seat and a smile waiting for you at Samson's.
